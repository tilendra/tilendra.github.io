Hello

       My name is Tilendra Choudhary, a post-doctoral data scientist and researcher at Duke University , Durham, NC with a focus on data science, signal processing, and machine learning. Before here, I have worked closely with Emory + GeorgiaTech Univ and Brigosha Technologies (R&D). As an AI enthusiast, I’m endlessly fascinated by new technologies and innovations in the fields of electronics and computer science. My current areas of research and development include physiological signal analysis, bioinformatics, AI and ML for healthcare and for other domains. Apart from research articles, I always enjoy reading spiritual scriptures .
